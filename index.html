Let's Progress Where Progress Meets Potential...!!

Recognizing the common aspiration for high performance among college students, we understand the challenges arising from inadequate analysis of there performance. Through our platform, users not only gain valuable insights but also have the opportunity to build a robust profile by adding certificates and other career- building elements. This also benefits colleges to effectively track and manage student records. So, join us in the journey of academic advancement and professional growth with Student Performance Analysis.

Created by third-year students from the Department of Emerging Technologies at SB Jain Institute of Technology Management and Research, Nagpur. Let's Progress is our ambitious 6th-semester Mini Project. Amid various project ideas, we chose this endeavour with a singular goal to elevate the academic performance of engineering students.
